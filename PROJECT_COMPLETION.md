Most ag-tech apps use weather proxies. We provide actual groundwater depth with regional calibration.

### 2. Interactive Visualizations
Farmers can tap to explore soil layers and understand water availability at different depths.

### 3. Complete Drought Prevention System
From risk assessment → crop selection → irrigation schedule → cost optimization.

### 4. Pre-loaded AP Data
17 regions with baseline groundwater data - works offline.

### 5. AI-Generated Strategies
Unique mitigation strategies based on specific drought level and region characteristics.
